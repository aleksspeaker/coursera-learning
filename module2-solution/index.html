Chicken

Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world,[1] and was one of the first domesticated animals. Chicken is a major worldwide source of meat and eggs for human consumption.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. Beef can be harvested from bulls, cows, heifers or steers. Its acceptability as a food source varies in different parts of the world.

Sushi

Sushi is the Japanese preparation and serving of cooked vinegared rice combined with varied ingredients (ネタ neta), chiefly seafood, vegetables, meat, and occasionally tropical fruits.
